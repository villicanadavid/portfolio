Georeferenced Rainwater Harvesting Systems Dashboard for Mexico City (2022)

This project aims to develop a comprehensive dashboard using LookerStudio to visualize georeferenced data on rainwater harvesting systems across Mexico City. The dashboard provides interactive maps and insightful visualizations to showcase the distribution and utilization of rainwater harvesting infrastructure throughout the city. By leveraging LookerStudio's powerful analytics capabilities, the project enables users to explore various metrics such as the number of systems installed, their capacities, geographic distribution, and potential environmental impact. Additionally, the dashboard may incorporate features for filtering data by district, analyzing trends over time, and comparing different types of harvesting systems. Through this project, stakeholders and policymakers can gain valuable insights into the status and effectiveness of rainwater harvesting initiatives in Mexico City, facilitating informed decision-making for sustainable water management practices.
